Peanut Butter and Banana Sandwich

Main Page Bacon Burgers Breakfast Smoothies

The best kind of sandwich!

This is the perfect snack when you are working hard and using a lot of energy, because it's packed with carbs and protein to fuel your body.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of multi-grain bread
  • enough peanut butter to cover both slices of bread
  • 1 banana sliced into coins

Steps

  1. toast bread
  2. spread peanut butter on both slices of bread
  3. spread banana slices evenly on one slice of bread and cover with the other slice
  4. Enjoy!
